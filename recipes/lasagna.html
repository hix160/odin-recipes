Lasgna

Ingredients:

  • 1 1/2lb. ground beed
  • 1 lb. hot breakfast sausage
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 cans (14.5 ounces) whole tomatoes
  • 2 cans (6 ounces) tomato paste
  • 4 Tbsp. dried parsley, divided
  • 2 Tbsp. dried basil
  • 2 1/2 tsp. salt
  • 3 c. lowfat cottage cheese
  • 2 whole beaten eggs
  • 1/2 c. grated (not shredded) Parmesan cheese
  • 1 Tbsp. olive oil
  • 1 package (10 ounces) lasagna noodles
  • 1 lb. sliced mozzarella cheese

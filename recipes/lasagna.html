Lasgna

Description:

Lasagna is a classic Italian dish that consists of layers of pasta, meat sauce, cheese, and béchamel sauce (or ricotta mixture). This recipe provides a straightforward approach to making a delicious, hearty lasagna that’s perfect for family dinners or gatherings.

Ingredients:

  • For the Meat Sauce:
    • 1 pound (450g) ground beef
    • 1 small onion, finely chopped
    • 2 cloves garlic, minced
    • 1 can (28 oz or 800g) crushed tomatoes
    • 1 can (6 oz or 170g) tomato paste
    • 1 can (15 oz or 425g) tomato sauce
    • 1/2 cup (120ml) water
    • 2 tablespoons sugar
    • 1 teaspoon dried basil
    • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
    • Salt and pepper to taste
  • For the Cheese Mixture:
    • 15 oz (425g) ricotta cheese
    • 1 egg
    • 1/2 teaspoon salt
    • 2 cups (200g) shredded mozzarella cheese
    • 3/4 cup (75g) grated Parmesan cheese
  • For Assembly:
    • 12 lasagna noodles
    • 1 cup (100g) shredded mozzarella cheese (for topping)
    • 1/4 cup (25g) grated Parmesan cheese (for topping)

Steps

  1. Prepare the Meat Sauce:
    • In a large skillet, cook the ground beef over medium heat until browned. Drain excess fat.
    • Add the chopped onion and garlic to the skillet. Cook until the onion is translucent.
    • Stir in the crushed tomatoes, tomato paste, tomato sauce, and water. Add sugar, basil, oregano, salt, and pepper. Simmer uncovered for about 30 minutes, stirring occasionally.

  2. Cook the Lasagna Noodles:
    • While the sauce is simmering, cook the lasagna noodles according to the package instructions. Drain and set aside.

  3. Prepare the Cheese Mixture:
    • In a medium bowl, combine ricotta cheese, egg, and salt. Mix well.
    • Stir in the shredded mozzarella and grated Parmesan cheese.

  4. Assemble the Lasagna:
    • Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
    • In a 9x13 inch (23x33 cm) baking dish, spread 1 cup of meat sauce on the bottom.
    • Place 4 lasagna noodles over the sauce.
    • Spread one-third of the ricotta cheese mixture over the noodles.
    • Top with 1 cup of meat sauce.
    • Repeat layers twice, ending with the remaining meat sauce.
    • Sprinkle the remaining 1 cup of shredded mozzarella and 1/4 cup of grated Parmesan cheese on top.

  5. Bake the Lasagna:
    • Cover the baking dish with aluminum foil (to prevent sticking, you can either spray the foil with cooking spray or ensure it doesn't touch the cheese).
    • Bake in the preheated oven for 25 minutes.
    • Remove the foil and bake for an additional 25 minutes, or until the lasagna is bubbly and the cheese is golden brown.
    • Let the lasagna cool for about 15 minutes before serving to allow it to set.

Pizza recipe

Pizza

Description:

Pizza is a beloved dish that originated in Italy and has become a global favorite. This simple pizza recipe features a homemade dough and a classic combination of tomato sauce, mozzarella cheese, and your choice of toppings. It's perfect for a fun cooking activity and a delicious meal.

Ingredients:

  • For the Dough:
    • 2 1/4 teaspoons (1 packet) active dry yeast
    • 1 1/2 cups (360ml) warm water (about 110째F or 45째C)
    • 3 1/2 to 4 cups (440-500g) all-purpose flour
    • 2 tablespoons olive oil
    • 2 teaspoons salt
    • 1 teaspoon sugar
  • For the Sauce:
    • 1 can (15 oz or 425g) tomato sauce
    • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
    • 1 teaspoon dried basil
    • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
    • 1/2 teaspoon onion powder
    • Salt and pepper to taste
  • For the Toppings:
    • 2 cups (200g) shredded mozzarella cheese
    • Additional toppings of your choice (pepperoni, mushrooms, bell peppers, onions, olives, etc.)

Steps

  1. Prepare the Dough:
    • In a large bowl, dissolve the yeast and sugar in the warm water. Let it sit for about 5 minutes, until it becomes frothy.
    • Add 2 cups of flour, olive oil, and salt to the yeast mixture. Stir until combined.
    • Gradually add the remaining flour, 1/2 cup at a time, until the dough comes together and is no longer sticky.
    • Transfer the dough to a floured surface and knead for about 5-7 minutes, until smooth and elastic.
    • Place the dough in a lightly oiled bowl, cover with a damp cloth, and let it rise in a warm place for about 1-1.5 hours, or until doubled in size.

  2. Prepare the Sauce:
    • In a medium bowl, combine the tomato sauce, oregano, basil, garlic powder, onion powder, salt, and pepper. Mix well and set aside.

  3. Preheat the Oven:
    • Preheat your oven to 475째F (245째C). If you have a pizza stone, place it in the oven to preheat as well.

  4. Assemble the Pizza:
    • Punch down the risen dough and divide it into two equal portions (for two pizzas).
    • On a floured surface, roll out one portion of the dough into a circle, about 12 inches in diameter. Transfer it to a piece of parchment paper for easy handling.
    • Spread a thin layer of the prepared sauce over the dough, leaving a small border around the edges.
    • Sprinkle half of the shredded mozzarella cheese evenly over the sauce.
    • Add your desired toppings.

  5. Bake the Pizza:
    • If using a pizza stone, transfer the pizza (with the parchment paper) onto the preheated stone. If not, place the pizza on a baking sheet.
    • Bake in the preheated oven for about 10-12 minutes, or until the crust is golden brown and the cheese is bubbly and slightly browned.
    • Remove the pizza from the oven and let it cool for a few minutes before slicing and serving.

  6. Repeat for Second Pizza:
    • Repeat steps 4 and 5 with the second portion of dough and remaining ingredients.
